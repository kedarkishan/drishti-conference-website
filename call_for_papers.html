Call for Papers

Overview

DRISHTI 2024 invites original research papers from students, research scholars, faculty, and industry participants. The conference covers a wide range of topics in management, engineering, science, and technology. Please follow the guidelines below for paper submission:

Paper Submission Guidelines
  • Papers must be written in English and should adhere to the specified format.
  • All submissions must be original and should not have been published elsewhere.
  • Authors should submit their papers through the online submission system by the deadline.
  • Each paper will be peer-reviewed by at least two reviewers.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.
